Department: MUSC
Course Number: 545B
Section: 001
Description: Advanced study of conducting techniques and of representative major compositions and literature (later historical periods). Emphasis on knowledge and conducting skills that can communicate a thorough understanding of genre, idiom, form, texture, and style, in rehearsal and performance.
Pre-reqs: (Restricted to M.Mus. Conducting (Choral, Orchestral, Wind) or permission of the instructor.)
Credits: 4
Mode of Delivery: In-Person
Requires in Person Attendance: Yes
Instructor: GIRARD, JONATHAN
