Department: NURS

Description: Clinical practice to integrate and consolidate professional nursing knowledge, skills and competencies in the care of adults/older adults experiencing acute health challenges and their families. Co-reqs: All of NURS 331, NURS 322.
Credits: 7
Mode of Delivery: In-Person
Requires in Person Attendance: Yes
