Department: BMEG
Course Number: 230
Section: 101
Description: Integrating the study of two dimensional movement of animals and humans with the study of the manner in which forces on biomechanical structures such as bones, ligaments, tendons, muscles and joints cause motion. Techniques of solid mechanics and two dimensional rigid body dynamics. Students who have received credit for MECH 435 cannot also receive credit for BMEG 230 or BMEG 330.
Pre-reqs: Either (a) all of BMEG 101, PHYS 170 or (b) all of APSC 101, BMEG 200, PHYS 170.
