Department: MATH
Course Number: 254
Section: 201
Description: Partial differentiation, extreme values, multiple integration, vector fields, line and surface integrals, the divergence and Stokes' theorems; applications to fluid mechanics: buoyancy, hydrostatic force, pipe flow, drag; applications to thermodynamics: work, entropy, heat transfer; numerical methods. Emphasis on mechanical engineering applications.
Pre-reqs: MATH 101 and one of MATH 152, MATH 221, MATH 223.
Co-reqs: All of MECH 222, MECH 225.
Credits: 3
Mode of Delivery: In-Person
Requires in Person Attendance: Yes
Term: 2, Days: Mon, Start Time: 8:00, End Time: 9:00
Instructor: WALLS, PATRICK
